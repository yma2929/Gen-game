About Us

We are two university students majoring in Artificial Intelligence and Software Engineering. We aim to combine AI with new programming techniques to create a unique and interactive gaming experience.

Our idea is to build a gaming platform that uses AI to create personalized virtual worlds for each player, based on their likes and interests. The platform will offer both solo and team challenges that adjust to each player’s skill level,so we can create teams based on player’s level. It will also include instant translation to help players from different cultures communicate easily.

Our goal is to transform the gaming industry by using AI to create new and exciting experiences. This supports Vision 2030’s goal of growing the digital entertainment sector and encouraging tech innovation
